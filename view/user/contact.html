Frequently Asked Questions

How does UniRecommend work?

Our AI-powered system analyzes your academic profile, interests, and goals to recommend universities that best match your preferences.

Is UniRecommend free to use?

Yes! Creating an account and getting basic recommendations is completely free. Premium features may be available for advanced matching.

How accurate are the recommendations?

Our recommendations are based on comprehensive data analysis and machine learning algorithms, with high accuracy rates validated by successful student placements.

Can I apply through UniRecommend?

We provide guidance and resources for applications, but you'll typically need to apply directly through university websites or application portals.
